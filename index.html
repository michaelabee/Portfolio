About Me

I'm a graduate of the University of Minnesota Full Stack Web Development Boot Camp and full stack web developer based in St. Paul, MN. I enjoy creating dynamic, user-friendly interfaces using Javascript and React. I also regularly work with Node, MongoDB and Express. I'm endlessly amazed at how important each element of a webpage is to the feeling it elicits from the user. I have developed MERN stack applications on my own and in a team environment, and often found myself in a leadership role within those groups.

I am searching for a Junior Web Development position working with React in the Twin Cities, but am also open to relocation within the next year. I want to be working under a mentor and to continue to develop my front end web development skills.

When I'm not coding I spend my time traveling, hiking and camping, playing violin or enjoying live music.
